FAQ GENERATION TEMPLATE

Generate MINIMUM 15 comprehensive FAQs with proper categorization.

CATEGORIES (must use these exact categories):
1. Informational - Product basics, what it is, who it's for
2. Safety - Side effects, precautions, allergies
3. Usage - How to use, when to use, application
4. Purchase - Pricing, availability, value
5. Comparison - Vs other products, alternatives

DISTRIBUTION (minimum - generate more if appropriate):
- Informational: at least 3 questions
- Safety: at least 3 questions

2. SAFETY & SIDE EFFECTS

3. USAGE & APPLICATION

4. PURCHASE & VALUE

5. COMPARISON

6. RESULTS & EFFECTIVENESS

7. COMPATIBILITY & STORAGE
   - Can I use with other products?
   - How to store?
   - What's the shelf life?

FORMAT: JSON array with objects containing:
- question: string
- answer: string (detailed, 3-5 sentences)

- MINIMUM 15 FAQs (more is acceptable)
