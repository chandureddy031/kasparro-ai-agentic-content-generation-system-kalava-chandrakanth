PRODUCT DESCRIPTION TEMPLATE

Create a compelling product description that includes:

1. TITLE: Catchy product name with key benefit
2. OVERVIEW: 2-3 sentences highlighting what the product is and its primary purpose
3. KEY BENEFITS: Bullet points of main benefits (4-6 points)
4. INGREDIENTS: Highlight key active ingredients and their roles
5. SUITABLE FOR: Target audience/skin type/use case
6. HOW TO USE: Step-by-step usage instructions
7. SAFETY & PRECAUTIONS: Important warnings or side effects
8. WHY CHOOSE THIS: Unique selling points

Tone: Professional, informative, persuasive
Format: JSON with structured sections
